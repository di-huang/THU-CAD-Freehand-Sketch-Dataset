The previous version of Abstract: The integration of artificial intelligence (AI) and Computer-Aided Design (CAD) becomes increasingly promising. Freehand sketches and corresponding CAD models are important modalities in data-driven algorithms of AI-based CAD applications, such as drawing-based CAD model retrieval. However, datasets with these paired modalities are rare, and most existing sketch datasets for CAD models either lack a freehand style or are insufficient. Thus, we propose a large-scale dataset of paired freehand sketches and 3D CAD models, the FS3D dataset. The dataset can be easily expanded using our data construction pipeline. Mainstream tasks based on freehand sketches and CAD models are performed on our dataset, pointing out the limitations of current models to these tasks. We release the dateset for facilitating the development of AI-based CAD research and applications.

large scale & dynamic

Our dataset provides dynamic annotated data for large scale indoor scene which contains amount of dynamic objects that present significant challenges for robot tasks.

scene understanding

Our dataset supports training and testing for various robotic scene understanding tasks (object detection, semantic segmentation, robot relocalization, scene reconstruction, etc.)

selective focus

Our dataset contains both real and synthetic annotated data, the expansion of its size and capabilities has great potential in the future.

Rich labels

Multiple labels such as instance segmentation, semantic segmentation, 3D/2D object detection, Depth, RGB, pose, etc., widely applicable in various fields.
